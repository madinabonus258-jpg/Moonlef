🌱 Chapter I — Greetings & Introductions

Learn the most common greetings and introductions in English. After this lesson, you'll be able to greet people, introduce yourself and say goodbye politely.

📚 Vocabulary

Word Meaning Example
Hello Greeting Hello! Nice to meet you.
Hi Informal greeting Hi, Emma!
Good morning Morning greeting Good morning, teacher.
Good afternoon Afternoon greeting Good afternoon, everyone.
Good evening Evening greeting Good evening, sir.
Good night Before sleeping Good night! Sleep well.
Goodbye Farewell Goodbye! See you tomorrow.
Bye Informal goodbye Bye!
See you later Farewell See you later!
Nice to meet you First meeting Nice to meet you!
My name is... Introduction My name is Anna.
I'm... Short introduction I'm David.
What's your name? Question What's your name?
How are you? Question How are you today?
I'm fine, thank you. Answer I'm fine, thank you.
Please Polite word Please sit down.
Thank you Gratitude Thank you very much.
You're welcome Reply You're welcome!
Sorry Apology Sorry I'm late.
Excuse me Getting attention Excuse me, where is the station?

💬 Example Dialogue

Anna: Hello!

Tom: Hi!

Anna: What's your name?

Tom: My name is Tom.

Anna: Nice to meet you!

Tom: Nice to meet you too!

💡 Tips

✅ Use Hello with everyone.

✅ Use Hi with friends.

✅ Use Good night before sleeping.

✅ Use Excuse me to get someone's attention.

⚠ Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Good night! (when meeting someone)
  • ✅ Good evening!

  • ❌ My name Tom.
  • ✅ My name is Tom.

  • ❌ I fine.
  • ✅ I'm fine.

📝 Summary

👋 Greet people politely.

😊 Ask "How are you?"

🙋 Introduce yourself.

🙏 Say "Thank you".

👋 Say goodbye politely.
